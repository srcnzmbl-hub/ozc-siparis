Privacy Policy

Ophanark (by Ozark Collective) · Last updated: 20 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Ophanark (“we”, “us”, “the app”), operated by Ozark Collective, collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the Ophanark app or the ozarkcollective.co website, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address and password you use to create an account. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are stored only in encrypted (hashed) form — we never see or store your plaintext password.
  • Preferences: your chosen app language and basic settings, stored to give you a consistent experience across your devices.
  • Content you submit: if you use features such as coffee-cup or palm reading, the photos you upload are processed to generate a reading. Birth details you enter (date, time, place) are used to calculate your natal chart.
  • Technical data: standard information such as app version and general device/browser type, used to keep the service working and secure.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and let you sign in across the website and the app.
  • To provide the app’s features (natal chart, readings, horoscope, chat) and your chosen language.
  • To operate, maintain, improve and protect the service.
  • To contact you about your account or important service notices.

The readings and interpretations offered in the app are for entertainment and reflection. They are not professional advice.

3. Service providers

We use trusted third-party providers to run the service, and share only what is necessary for them to function:

  • Supabase — account authentication and secure data storage.
  • GitHub Pages — website and app hosting.
  • Zoho Mail — sending account and confirmation emails.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not show third-party advertising.

4. Data retention & your rights

  • We keep your account data for as long as your account is active.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • To delete your account and associated data, contact us at the address below and we will process your request.

5. Children

Ophanark is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.

7. Contact

For any privacy question or request, contact us at: hello@ozarkcollective.co
