Frequently asked questions

What is a birth chart poster?

A birth chart (natal chart) poster maps the exact position of the sun, moon and planets at the moment you were born, rendered as celestial wall art and personalized with your name.

What do I need to order?

Your name, plus your birth date, exact time and city. We calculate your chart from those details using precise astronomy.

Is it printed or a digital download?

It's a made-to-order, print-ready digital download. You receive a high-resolution file you can print at any size, from A4 up to 24×36".

Can I get a couple compatibility chart?

Yes — the couple synastry chart blends both partners' birth charts into a single design, a popular anniversary and wedding gift.

Which languages are available?

Your poster text can be printed in 40+ languages, including English, Spanish, German, French, Russian and Ukrainian.
